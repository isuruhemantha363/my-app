How to Stay Connected

  • When you receive an order, you will be notified via SMS. You can then view the order details through our App.
  • If you can accept the order, press 'Confirm'; otherwise, press 'Reject'. We expect a quick response from you.
  • Due to technical issues, SMS notifications may sometimes fail, so please stay connected with the App at all times.
  • You can log in to your Profile page using the Email and Password provided during registration, where all your order messages will be displayed.
  • Stay updated on your orders. Customers may cancel orders, and all such updates will be reflected in the App.
  • While cancellation alerts are sent via SMS, please check the App regularly as technical errors may prevent SMS delivery.
